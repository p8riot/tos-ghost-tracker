Notes
Hearing heartbeat during hunt = Ghost sees you. RUN! Cellphone, including phone flashlight, can safely be used during hunt. Turn off or drop all other electronics you're carrying. Ghosts will have a favorite room but they can leave evidence anywhere inside the location. Infrasound Receiver has to be in favorite room to capture audio. Forced hunts from Holy Water (Demon, Tariaksuq, Wisp) execute at the specified time if any player is inside the location. Forced hunts pause the regular hunt cooldown. If no players are inside, the forced hunt is skipped and normal cooldown resumes. The hunt threshold for all ghosts (except Demon) is 104 BPM. This threshold is the average BPM of all players inside the location. Players outside the location with a low heart rate do not help prevent a hunt.
